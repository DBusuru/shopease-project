Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to be logged in to complete your purchase.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, PayPal, and M-Pesa payments.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also check your order status in your account dashboard.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Please contact our customer service team for detailed information.
